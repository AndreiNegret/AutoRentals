FAQ's

Frequently asked questions of our customers.

Payment

A&S Car Rental currently offers two (2) methods of confirming a reservation. We offer a "Reserve Free Cancellation" option in which you pay at the time of making the reservation but you can receive a refund if you cancel 24 hours prior to your scheduled arrival. We also offer a "Reserve Pay On Arrival" option. This allows you to book a vehicle without prepaying at the time of making your reservation.
In most cases, if you return a vehicle early, we will recalculate the rate and charge you according to the length of time you had the vehicle. However, some locations do charge an early return fee. You will need to review the location's policies to confirm.
Each of our locations are independently owned and operated, you will need to review the location's policies to review the forms of payment it will accept. In addition some locations do have a specific policy for local renters.

Preparation

All drivers must possess a valid drivers license issued in their country of residence, and an acceptable means of payment as identified in the Location Policies. Some locations have additional requirements and restrictions, please check the Location Policies for your specific rental location.
To change an existing reservation you will need to either contact ACE Rent A Car for assistance or cancel your original reservation and rebook a new reservation. Please note that a new rental rate may apply when changes are made.
The renter is responsible for any damage to the vehicle or liability that occurs during the rental period. ACE locations offer Collision Damage Waiver and Supplemental Liability Insurance that release you from that responsibility. Your personal car insurance, homeowners' policy, or credit card supplements may provide some coverage. Please check with your insurance agent before your travel date. If you incur damages or liability not covered by your insurance, you will be personally responsible for those costs.
All A&S Car Rental locations rent to drivers at least 25 years of age. If under the age of 25, please refer to the location's policy. Fees and vehicle restrictions may apply.

During the Rental

We understand your plans may change and you may need to keep the vehicle longer than planned. If this occurs, please contact the rental location directly via the phone number provided on the rental agreement.
Your safety is our top concern. If you experience an accident or breakdown, please contact the rental office using the phone number printed on your rental agreement as soon as possible.

After the Rental

Contact the location directly via the phone number provided on your rental agreement.
Please reach out to our customer care team via a&scarrental@gmail.com
